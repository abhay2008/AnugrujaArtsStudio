About us

Anuradha Govarthanan - A professional artist

About Artist

Anuradha Govarthanan, an engineer turned artist from Chennai, pursued her passion for art alongside family responsibilities. She ventured into fashion design, opened a boutique, and mastered watercolors. With more than 7 years of experience in art and teaching art and opening her studio followed, culminating in recognition. Her journey epitomizes the harmonious integration of family and artistic fulfillment.

Achievements

With more than 7 years of experience in art, Anuradha Govarthanan has received a lot of awards and success in her career.

• Best paintings award 2022 given by Kalakaar foundation, New Delhi.
• Sri PV Narasimha Rao Excellency Award 2022
• Finalist Award from Teravana international online juried exhibition 2024
• Golden Award from National all india completion by shiny colors, Bangalore
