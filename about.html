Anuradha Govardhan - An award winning artist

Information

About Life

Anuradha Govarthanan, an engineer turned artist from Chennai, pursued her passion for art alongside family responsibilities. She ventured into fashion design, opened a boutique, and mastered watercolors. Teaching art and opening her studio followed, culminating in recognition. Her journey epitomizes the harmonious integration of family and artistic fulfillment.

Achievements

Anuradha Govardhan has received a lot of awards and success in her career.

She has received Best paintings award 2022 given by Kalakaar foundation, New Delhi. She has also recieved Sri PV Narasimha Rao Excellency Award 2022

Exhibitions

• Eshwaraiya art gallery, Hyderabad
• State gallery of arts, Hyderabad
• Kalakaar Foundation, New Delhi
• Tanu Nabunkar, Siliguri
• Fabriano Aquarelle, Italy 2021
• Japan International watercolor Institute, Japan 2022

Art Workshops
