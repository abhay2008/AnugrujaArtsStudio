Online & Offline Classes

• Global Accessibility: Classes are conducted online, accommodating students worldwide at their preferred timings.
• Age-specific Schedule: Weekly classes: twice a week for kids under 15, and once a week for adults.
• Age Range: Open to ages 7 and above, extending to senior citizens.
• Real-life Subjects: Curriculum focuses on practical, real-world topics.
• Conceptual Learning: Emphasizes understanding concepts over rote memorization.
• Mediums by Age Group:
- Kids (below 15): Pencil, colored pencils, watercolor, soft pastel.
- Above 15: Pencil, charcoal, watercolor, acrylic, oil.
• Personalized Attention: Limited one-on-one sessions available for tailored instruction.

Watercolour courses

Develop your artistic watercolour skills

• Unzipping Watercolor Course
• Course Options: Choose between 3 months or 6 months duration.
• Course Content: Learn in-depth techniques of watercolors. Covers art fundamentals.
• Outcome: Gain confidence in handling the medium. Ability to paint in impressionistic and realistic styles.

Short term courses

Learn the fundementals of art and sketching

• Short Term Courses on Art Fundamental and Sketching
• Course Duration: 2 months.
• Course Content: Learn the basics of sketching and perspectives, both in theoretical and practical ways. Mediums covered: Charcoal and pencil.
